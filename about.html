Who is M.C GAIA?

M.C GAIA is a Toronto-based Rap artist that specializes in 90s-style rap- having inspiration from legends of the era to influence his style and rhythm. His breakout album "NOTORIUS", made waves across the internet, and made M.C GAIA one of the many Canadian Rappers to put Canada on the world stage! Currently, he has made 3 albums- NOTORIUS, Coast 2 Coast, and the newest addition: Gates of a New City.
